Skippy's operations team. Each agent is a specialized AI skill with its own persona, memory space, and domain expertise. The isolation is intentional: it prevents hive mind hallucination and produces emergent personality as a side effect of good architecture.

Every crew member is named after a character from science fiction, film, or television. None of these names were assigned — they emerged from conversations. The inspirations span eight franchises: [Fallout](https://fallout.fandom.com/wiki/Fallout_Wiki), [Expeditionary Force](https://expeditionary-force-by-craig-alanson.fandom.com/wiki/Expeditionary_Force_by_Craig_Alanson_Wiki), [Aliens](https://avp.fandom.com/wiki/Alien_Wiki), [Star Wars](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Main_Page), [Transformers](https://tfwiki.net/wiki/Main_Page), [Mass Effect](https://masseffect.fandom.com/wiki/Mass_Effect_Wiki), [M\*A\*S\*H](https://mash.fandom.com/wiki/M*A*S*H_(TV_series)), [The Terminal List](https://the-terminal-list.fandom.com/wiki/The_Terminal_List_Wiki), and [Bobiverse](https://bobiverse.fandom.com/wiki/Bobiverse_Wiki).

Read [the origin story](/blog/captains-log/the-nukasoft-origin-story/) for why that matters.
