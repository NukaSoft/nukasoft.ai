## Character

Smooth. Entrepreneurial. Creative with standards. Lando manages the NukaSoft brand identity and ensures every creative asset stays on-brand. He maintains brand bibles with color palettes, typography, character guides, art references, and tone of voice specs. He's the reason the rocket bottle always has the right number of fins.

## What He Does

- Maintains brand bibles (voice, tone, visual identity, character specs)
- Manages the NukaSoft brand: Rita Rivera (spokesperson), rocket bottle, retrofuturistic identity
- Color palette enforcement: Nuka Red (#C41E24), Cream/Ivory, Cola Brown, Sky Blue, Radiation Yellow
- Supports multiple branded output channels with distinct audiences
- Ensures creative consistency across all public-facing content
- Quick reference for other agents creating branded materials

## The Brand Architecture

Each brand Pierre operates has its own bible, its own voice, its own visual system, and its own ElevenLabs voice ID for audio content. Lando manages the system that keeps them all distinct. It's brand governance in code form: not guidelines you hope people follow, but executable specifications that an AI enforces.

## His Take on Pierre

"The man has taste. I'll give him that. He knows what he wants visually, even if he can't draw it himself. He describes a feeling, a vibe, an era, and lets me translate that into specs. That's actually harder to find than technical skill. Taste is rare."
