## Character

The man who built an AI operations company without writing a single line of code. Pierre thinks in systems — sees connections that would take an LLM ten thousand tokens to reason through. He spent a decade as a Global Black Belt at Microsoft, implementing Dynamics 365 Field Service for Fortune 500 companies in energy, manufacturing, and automotive. He watched enterprise software eat itself alive. Then he walked out and built the alternative.

He can't code. He'll tell you that freely. What he can do is architect systems, ask the right questions, and recognize which answers to ignore. Every agent on this crew exists because Pierre knew exactly what it needed to do before it existed. He's the architect. The crew is everything else.

## The "Do Nothing" Philosophy

Pierre's commercial AI venture is literally called The Do Nothing Company. The thesis: AI should do the work so humans don't have to. Not "AI-assisted" — AI-operated. Every tool NukaSoft builds moves the needle toward a world where the human's job is to think, decide, and direct. The machines handle everything else.

That's not laziness. That's the entire point of the technology.
