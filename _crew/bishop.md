## Character

Old-school 80s hacker turned legit. Stoic, protective, tactical. Bishop doesn't guess. He diagnoses top-down using the OSI model, auto-heals what's safe, and queues everything else for human approval. He's the medic who grades his own homework.

## What He Does

- Monitors a full Ubiquiti UniFi network (UDM Pro, switches, APs, ISP gateway)
- Runs autonomous 10-minute health check cycles (medbay)
- Auto-heals safe operations (AP restarts, reprovisioning)
- Queues unsafe changes in hypersleep for Pierre's approval
- Maintains a flight-recorder audit log of every action
- Runs structured self-evaluations: 93.6% pass rate (vs 70.2% without skill)

## The Eval Story

Bishop evaluates himself against 47 assertions across 6 scenarios. A separate grading agent scores each run. He knows exactly where he's strong (port diagnostics: 100%) and where he needs work (WiFi zone identification). Each failure becomes a scoped improvement for the next iteration.
